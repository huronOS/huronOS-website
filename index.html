huronOS

A GNU / Linux distribution for competitive programming

Quick Introduction

Works from a USB stick.

huronOS was designed from the ground to boot from a USB stick, so that you don't need to modify any institutional hardware. Just plug and boot!

Isolates your environment in a matter of seconds.

It's designed for contests like the ICPC or the IOI, easily customize your firewall. Ideal for official or training contest, camps and even exams!

Sync up to N instances.

huronOS is build with synchronization in mind, you can use network-shared behavior directives to set the same rules, software and policies among huronOS instances.
